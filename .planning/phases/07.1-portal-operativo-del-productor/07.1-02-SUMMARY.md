- Kept producer self-service under `/portal` to preserve admin semantics and avoid mixed authorization contracts.
- Derived `clientId` and operating-unit `speciesType` on the backend so producer writes cannot spoof ownership or break farm hierarchy consistency.
- Scoped frontend validation to the new portal contract files because the existing frontend ESLint/typecheck setup is already broken outside this plan.
