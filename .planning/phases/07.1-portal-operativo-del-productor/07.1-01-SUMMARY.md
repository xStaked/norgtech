# Phase 7.1 Plan 01: Producer Ownership and Operating Unit Foundation Summary

**Producer portal writes now bind to a unique authenticated client scope, and Prisma models a farm-to-operating-unit hierarchy ready for unit-level records.**

## Accomplishments
- Centralized producer ownership resolution in the auth guard and propagated the resolved `clientId` through request context.
- Removed the ambiguous single-active-client fallback from farm, case, and visit services, and forced producer mutations to use server-scoped ownership.
- Added `OperatingUnit` plus optional `operatingUnitId` references on farm-linked records with a migration that applies cleanly.
